Open-access mass-casualty incident triage tool

Triage Tag Explanation
Expectant (Black) Victim is deceased or is not expected to survive due to the severity of their injuries.
Immediate (Red) Victim has severe injuries requiring immediate treatment.
Delayed (Yellow) Victim has potentially severe injuries, but treatment can be delayed up to a few hours.
Minor (Green) Victim has minor injuries not requiring immediate treatment.
