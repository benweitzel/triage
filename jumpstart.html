Triage Tag Explanation
Expectant
 Victim is deceased or is not expected to survive due to the severity of their injuries.
Immediate
 Victim has severe injuries requiring immediate treatment.
Delayed
 Victim has potentially severe injuries, but treatment can be delayed up to a few hours.
Minor
 Victim has minor injuries not requiring immediate treatment.

Assess mental status using the AVPU scale. AVPU is an acronym for alert (patients who are awake), verbal (patient responds only to verbal stimuli), pain (patient responds only to painful stimuli), or unresponsive (patient does not respond to any stimuli). Appropriate responses to painful stimuli are purposeful movements, such as localizing or withdrawing from pain.
