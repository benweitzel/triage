Triage Tag Explanation
Expectant
 Victim is deceased or is not expected to survive due to the severity of their injuries.
Immediate
 Victim has severe injuries requiring immediate treatment.
Delayed
 Victim has potentially severe injuries, but treatment can be delayed up to a few hours.
Minor
 Victim has minor injuries not requiring immediate treatment.

Capillary refill time is determined by applying pressure to a finger (often the nail bed) and assessing the length of time it takes for the skin to return to normal color. In adults, this should be approximately 2 seconds or less. An alternative is assessing the presence of a radial pulse. A long capillary refill time or absent radial pulse may be an indicator of poor perfusion, often caused by blood loss.
